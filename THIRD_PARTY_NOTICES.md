# Third-party notices

The weekly price observations were published in the `data` directory of the public repository:

- Yuya-Hikima/AAAI25-Zeroth-Order-Methods-for-Nonconvex-Stochastic-Problems-with-Decision-Dependent-Distributions

That repository states that the observations originate from the KSP-SP “New Product Sales Ranking” data. Only the first ten entries of each weekly file affect this benchmark, but the complete 50-entry files are included for traceability.

The method definitions and experimental constants are based on:

- Yuya Hikima, Hiroshi Sawada, and Akinori Fujino, “Guided Zeroth-Order Methods for Stochastic Non-convex Problems with Decision-Dependent Distributions,” ICML 2025.
- The authors’ public implementation repository, `Yuya-Hikima/ICML2025-Guided-Zeroth-Order-Methods`.

This package is an independently structured implementation. Third-party data and publications remain subject to their original terms.
